TWO LINES SHAYRIS

Do Ashaar mein itni taasir honi chaiye ke wo poori kahani bayaan kar dein!!
-Faisal Khatri.

PreviousNextClose

Mohabbat

Raabtay tak nahi hote dar asal unse,
Khwab mein jinka aana jana musalsal ho...!! 

Waada

Aitbaar ki hadd to ye thi ke harf e takalluff par itminaan paa gaye,
Tera Waada magar waada raha, Hum intezaar mein duniya se rukhsati paa gaye...! 

Suhani Raatein

Raatein suhani, baatein purani,
Na yaad dilaiye wo zamana mohabbat ka...!! 

Muqaam

Aisa hargiz nahi hai ke teri jagah koi aur aa gaya,
Magar tera wo maqaam bhi ab tera nahi raha...!! 

Muntazir Nigahein

Muntazir nigahein dekh rahi hain rasta tera,
Kabhi keh gaye thay, ke aayenge milne kisi shab..!! 

Dhokebaaz

Dhokebaaz kabhi nahi badalta,
Sirf uski dhoka dene ki tarkeebein badalti hai...!! 

Waqt

Waqt jab badalta hai na,
To Rolex pehanne wale bhi roti ko taras jaate hain..!


Wo bhi kya daur tha

Bahot yaad aate hain wo din mujhko,
Jab be-rok tok baatein hum kiya karte thay,
Kuch tum gungunati thi, kuch shair hum kaha karte thay...!! 

Ishq

Kahaniya'n to bahot hain ishq ki magar,
wo jo anjaam tak pahunche wo bahot kam hain...! 

Haadse

Kab, kaun, kahan chhor jaye,
Tum soch bhi nahi sakte haadse kaise kaise pesh aa sakte hain..!! 

Bewafai

Wohi ishq, wohi junoon, wohi mohabbat meri aaj bhi hai,
Sanam bass tere pyaar ka zaika badal gaya...!! 

Love Aajkal

Kya ghazab hai iss zamane ka trend,
I Love you, but as a friend. 

Faaslein

Faasle badha diye hain apno ne,
Aur ilzaam humpar lagate hain ke mulakaat nahi karte..!! 

Dard

Waqt khud ilaaj hai chand zakhmo'n ka,
Ye dard bhi mehmaan hai chand lamho'n ka...!! 

Kismat

Yahan kismat calendar badalne ke mood mein nahi lag rahi hai,
Aur log pooch rahe hain 31st ka kya plan hai...!! 

Wafa

Nafsa nafsee ka aalam hai,
Adakaar to bahot milte hain, Wafadaar nahi milta..!! 

Gila

Jo bolta tha to gila tha,
Ab khamosh hu to chain nahi padta unhein...! 

Purana Dard

Waqt ka kya hai, badal jayega,
Purana dard magar reh jayega...!! 

Apne

Jab waqt apna na rahe,
To apne bhi kahan apne rehte hain...!! 

Badalta Waqt

Waqt bura chal raha hai,
Magar badalna iski fitrat hai..!! 

Himmat

Taqaza ye kehta hai aftaab doobne wala hai,
Himmat ye kehti hai, Ek daanv aur aazma ke dekh..!! 

Zindagi

Rahat se jeene de ae zindagi ab do pal to,
Umr bhar to tere ishaare pe nachte aaye hain...!! 

Shair o Sukhan

Ye shair o sukhan ka karobaar apna,
Munafa na sahi, dil ko sukoon zaroor milta hai..! 

Kirdaar

Itna to yakeen hai kirdaar par apne,
Koi khafa to ho sakta hai, bhula nahi sakta..!!

Khwab

Thakk gaya hu zindagi teri dushwariya'n seh kar,
So jata hu roz khwab purane lekar..!!

Kasam

Saath mein jee bhi na sake tum to,
Saath marne ki kasmein khaate thay..!

Matlabi

Bass matlab ke din aane do,
bhoolne wale bhi yaad karne lagenge...!!

Badla

Badla lene ki zarurat nahi mujhko,
Jisne mujhe dukh diya hai, wo mujhe waise bhi kho chuke hain...!!

Talash

Agar, magar aur kaash mein biit gayi,
Ye zindagi khudki talash mein biit gayi..!! 

Aarzoo

Bada soona soona sa lagta hai tere bina,
Na koi aarzoo na koi tamanna tere bina...!!
