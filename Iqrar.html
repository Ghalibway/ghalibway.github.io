Iqrar

Lamha lamha sochna tumhe,
Chup chup kar dekhna tumhe,

Kabhi khayalo’n mein guftugu tumse,
Kabhi tasawwur mein mulakaatein tumse..

Wo din, wo yaadein, wo college ki baatein,
Bhulaye kahan bhulengi wo chandni raatein..

Shab bhar tera intezaar karna,
Dil hi dil mein tumse izhar karna..

Waqt guzar gaya, Mausam badal gaya,
Main ab bhi wohi hu “faisal”, mera kaam tere ishq ka iqrar karna…!! 

-Faisal Khatri
